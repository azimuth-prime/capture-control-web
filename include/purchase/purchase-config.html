Purchase Order Configuration

Configure workflows and settings for Purchase Orders.

Automation
Configure how Purchase Order automation is handled.
Indicate whether purchase orders should be auto generated based on stock level counts. In cases where a SKU has a stock threshold defined, a Purchase Order will be created when that threshold is reached. Otherwise Purchase Orders will be automatically created based on backorder count.
In cases where the SKU is supplied by a single vendor that vendor will be used for the Purchase Order. In other cases there may be more than one vendor in which case indicate below the rules for auto selection.
Communications
Configure how communications for purchase orders are handled by the system.
Enable feature to auto email purchase orders to supplier when it is "Issued".
Purchase Order Invoicing
Configure how invoicing ans costs are calculated on Purchase Orders.
Require a supplier invoice to be added before allowing items to be received.
Configure when and if landed costs are calculated and recorded in your QuickBooks COGs account.
Configure how landed costs are applied across items in a Purchase Order.
