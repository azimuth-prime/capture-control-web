The inventory audit process assists you in completing a full inventory audit of a physical warehouse. When the audit is created, it will remain open until complete. You can cancel an audit at any time by clicking the "Cancel Audit" button the audit details screen. After an audit is created, it will be populated with all inventory items that have stock within the physical warehouse. You can either print audit pages containing count sheets or use a mobile or tablet device to update values in real-time.

The audit system will track your progress as you update inventory values. You can come back to an open audit at any time and continue your count.
